About Me

For years, I've had paint on my hands and curiosity in my heart. What started as a love for acrylic painting on canvas slowly expanded into the world of digital technology — and eventually, into the fascinating universe of artificial intelligence.

I've always believed that creativity has no boundaries. Whether I'm mixing colors on a real canvas or sculpting prompts for a neural network, the feeling is the same — something magical is about to happen.

Today, I combine traditional art with digital creation. I watch characters come to life from a simple idea, see imaginary worlds take shape through AI tools, and discover new ways to tell visual stories every single day. There is nothing quite like the moment an AI-generated character moves for the first time — watching a static idea take its first breath never gets old!

I live in Slovakia and love exploring Europe — each journey brings new inspiration for colors, stories, and characters. My little hedgehogs, a lonely saxophonist, and dancing fairies all carry pieces of places and moments I've collected along the way.

I share my work on Facebook and Instagram, and I write about the creative process right here on this blog — how content is made, which tools I use, what works and what hilariously doesn't. I’ve spent countless hours 'negotiating' with AI models to get the physics just right—so you don't have to.

  • Behind-the-scenes of how animated reels are created from scratch
